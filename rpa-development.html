RPA stands for robotic process automation, also called software robotics, which uses advanced intelligent automation technologies to streamline time-consuming tasks, eliminate errors, and enhance business efficiency more efficiently. It simplifies various tasks, including transferring files, extracting data, submitting forms, and more. Robotic Process Automation (RPA) mimics the way humans interact with screen elements without having to see the screen.
