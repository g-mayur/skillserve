UI/UX design and web design focus on different aspects of a website or app: web design focuses mainly on the visual appearance of the website, on the other hand, UI/UX design focuses on the user experience. User experience and user interface designers want to create something that is not only visually appealing or attractive but also makes the user's desired behaviors a reality.
