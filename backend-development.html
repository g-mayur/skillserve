Frequently Asked Questions

Backend development involves the process of developing and maintaining all the server-side aspects, including database, server, and application login. We understand how important it is to design and develop a well-structured and efficient backend system to ensure that an app runs smoothly and performs well even under heavy load. Therefore, backend development takes care of storing and organizing data while ensuring everything runs smoothly on the client side.

Crest Skillserve is a reputed software company that offers fast, reliable, and cost-efficient custom software development services including frontend and backend development based on specific business requirements. With our backend development service, we develop custom APIs, manage database interactions, and implement server-side logic to allow our front end to display dynamic content, process user inputs, and provide interactive features.

The cost of basic backend development may take around $5,000 to $15,000. Backend development uses numerous powerful backend programming languages, including Django, CakePHP, Laravel, Ruby on Rails, Node.js, Nest.js, and Spring Boot. For larger data & complex backend development, the cost may be higher between $40,000 and $100,000. However, the actual cost of backend development heavily depends on specific project requirements and customizations.
