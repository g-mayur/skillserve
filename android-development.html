Android app development cost totally depends on the app type, size, complexity, core features, platform selection, technologies, and the location of the app development partner. There is no predefined cost to build an Android app, it varies depending on a specific business needs and goals. For example, it would take between 1 to 3 months to develop a simple app with basic features and low complexity. Building an Android app with medium-scale and medium-complexity may take around 4 to 6 months. On the other hand, it will take about 7 months to design and build large-scale and high-complexity apps.

The overall cost of an Android app development depends on a number of features, including but not limited to app complexity, app size, app features, framework selection, and much more. It may cost somewhere between $5,000 to $10,000 to build a simple app with basic features. The cost of building a complex mobile application will be around $30,000-$90,000. However, this is just an estimated cost, the final cost may vary depending on your app requirements and goals.
